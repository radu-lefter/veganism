Calculate the impact of your diet

People often forget that every piece of nicely packaged meat with images of happy animals on it comes from an actually living being. Here's a calculator to help you put the impact of your diet into perspective.

You can introduce the amount of meat you eat per week in kilograms and the calculator will tell you how many animals are sacrificed per year. It also tells you how many animals you could save if you ate half as much meat. It doesn't have to be exact, an estimate is fine. Note that we will not save the results and they will be deleted as soon as the page refreshes.







As you can see, the results are very roughly estimated. Below you can find the data we used to calculate the results. They are based on the average weight of the carcass of a slaughtered animal. We used the most popular kinds of animals, there are many other kinds of animals that people consume, depending on the country and culture, even animals that you may consider pets. We aslo have to keep in mind that there are many breeds and that not all animals weigh the same.
